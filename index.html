Kaizen'21 is organizing a bunch of competitions and informal events with exciting cash prizes and other awards.









Climate Olympiad

Greetings from NSS IITD!
NSS IITD in collaboration with Climate Science (A London-based charity) at Kaizen'21 brings to you the first-ever global competition to find solutions to some climate-change-based problems by using teamwork & problem-solving skills. Qualifiers round will be held online during Kaizen. Qualifiers will compete further in Semi Finals and Finale. There's also a prize pool of $10,000 for the top three participants. Participate either solo or in a team.

Instructions for Registration

  1. Visit https://climatescience.org/olympiad/
    • Choose Private event - NSS IIT Delhi
    • Use Password - NSSOlympiad21
  2. Join WhatsApp Group (Mandatory)
    • https://linktr.ee/kaizen_CSOlympiad


Event Date: April 4, 2021
Event Time : 06:00 PM - 09:00 PM
Deadline to register 25th March 11:59 PM

Social Innovation Challenge

About 1 lakh tonnes of solid waste is generated in India per day. With increasing populations, changing policy requirements, new sustainability and recycling goals and improved technology departments, this is only going to overshoot unless something concrete is done about it. NSS IIT Delhi invites you to its Ideathon- a Social Innovation Challenge 3.0 under the aegis of Kaizen’21!
